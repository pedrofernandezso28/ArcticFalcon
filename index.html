04 — How we hunt

Four weeks to
the first real artifact.

No 90-day discovery phases. We ship a functioning prototype in the first sprint — because the only way to find the right product is to hold a wrong one in your hands.

One week pressure-testing the problem with your team. We come back with a scoped proposal or a clean "this isn't for us."

02

Dive

A cross-functional pod embeds. Architecture, designs and a functioning vertical slice — in four weeks or less.

03

Strike

Production build. Short sprints, hard deadlines, weekly shipping demos. You see everything we see.
